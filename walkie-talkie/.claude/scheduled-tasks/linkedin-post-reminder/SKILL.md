## TEMPLATE OPTIONS (rotate through these — pick a different one each time)

### 1. STORY + LESSON
> **Hook:** Start with a 1-sentence personal story ("Three years ago I almost quit. Here's what I learned.")
> **Body:** 3–5 short paragraphs telling the story with tension and resolution
> **Lesson:** "The lesson: [insight]"
> **CTA:** Ask a question to spark comments ("Have you ever felt this way?")

---

### 2. CONTRARIAN TAKE
> **Hook:** Challenge a common belief in your industry ("Everyone says X. I disagree.")
> **Why they're wrong:** 2–3 bullet points
> **Your perspective:** What you believe instead and why
> **CTA:** "Agree or disagree? Drop your take below."

---

### 3. HOW-TO / TACTICAL POST
> **Hook:** Promise a specific outcome ("How I [result] in [time/steps]")
> **Steps:** Numbered list (5–7 steps), each with one sentence of explanation
> **Closing:** What the outcome looks like once done
> **CTA:** "Save this for later. Which step do you find hardest?"

---

### 4. BEHIND THE SCENES
> **Hook:** Pull back the curtain ("Here's what building [thing] actually looks like")
> **Reality:** Share 3 raw, honest observations — the messy parts most people don't talk about
> **Reframe:** What these challenges taught you
> **CTA:** "What's the behind-the-scenes nobody talks about in your work?"

---

### 5. LIST POST (VALUE BOMB)
> **Hook:** "X things I wish I knew before [milestone/experience]"
> **List:** 5–10 punchy, specific items — no fluff
> **Closer:** One personal reflection tying it together
> **CTA:** "Which one resonates most with you?"

---

### 6. WIN + VULNERABILITY
> **Hook:** Share a recent win or milestone (be specific with numbers if possible)
> **The hard part:** What it actually cost — the doubt, the setbacks, the moments you wanted to stop
> **Gratitude:** Who or what helped you get there
> **CTA:** "What's a recent win you're proud of, big or small?"

---

### 7. PREDICTION / FUTURE TAKE
> **Hook:** "Here's what I think [industry/topic] will look like in [timeframe]"
> **Predictions:** 3 bold, specific predictions with reasoning
> **Your bet:** What you're personally doing about it
> **CTA:** "What's your prediction? Am I wrong?"

---

## YOUR TASK

1. **Pick the template** that you haven't used recently (or that fits today's energy/news).
2. **Draft the post** using the template — make it personal, specific, and authentic. Avoid generic advice.
3. **Check length:** Aim for 150–300 words. Short paragraphs, lots of white space.
4. **Review your hook:** The first line must stop the scroll. Rewrite it until it's impossible to ignore.
5. **Add hashtags:** 3–5 relevant hashtags at the end (not in the body).

Present the final draft clearly so it's ready to copy-paste into LinkedIn.
